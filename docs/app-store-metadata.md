## Long Description

**App Store / Google Play (4000 characters max):**

Algo is your AI-powered growth assistant, purpose-built for the Algonit marketing and sales automation platform. Get instant answers about your business performance, receive proactive growth recommendations, and take action on your campaigns -- all through natural conversation.

**Talk to Your Business Data**
Ask Algo anything about your marketing and sales performance. "How are my campaigns doing?" "Which leads should I follow up with?" "What's my conversion rate this week?" Algo understands your questions and delivers clear, actionable answers using real-time data from your Algonit account.

**Voice-First Experience**
Speak naturally to get instant insights. Algo converts your voice to text, processes your request with advanced AI, and reads the response back to you. Perfect for busy professionals who need answers on the go.

**Smart Growth Recommendations**
Algo proactively analyzes your business data and delivers personalized recommendations to help you grow. Get notified about high-value leads, campaign optimization opportunities, and performance milestones before you even ask.

**Real-Time Dashboard**
View your key business metrics at a glance. Algo's dashboard surfaces the numbers that matter most: active leads, campaign performance, conversion rates, and revenue trends. Tap any metric to dive deeper with AI-powered analysis.

**Take Action with Confidence**
Go beyond reading data. Tell Algo to send WhatsApp messages, schedule campaigns, update lead statuses, and more. Every action requires your confirmation before execution, so you stay in control.

**Proactive Alerts**
Never miss an important business event. Algo monitors your Algonit account and sends push notifications for new high-value leads, campaign milestones, performance anomalies, and time-sensitive opportunities.

**Key Features:**
- Natural language chat with AI (text and voice)
- Real-time business dashboard with key metrics
- AI-powered growth recommendations
- WhatsApp Business message management
- Campaign performance analytics
- Lead qualification and follow-up suggestions
- Proactive push notification alerts
- Action execution with confirmation flow
- Multi-turn contextual conversations
- Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication

**Requirements:**
- An active Algonit (AIMM) account (Free, Basic, or Business plan)
- Internet connection required

Built with enterprise-grade security: end-to-end encryption, JWT authentication with token rotation, tenant data isolation, and comprehensive audit logging.
